Anime Recommendation

Hello and Welcome!
This is a free to all anime recommendation engine setup by a college freshmen

The recommendation engine applied the technique and concept of stochastic gradient descent(SGD) in Machine Learning

The recommendation engine will generate recommend watchlist base on your rating along with others' rating on various anime.

Thus, the more anime you and others rate, the better the recommendation engine will perform

The primary purpose of this website is to recommend people animes that they will enjoy to watch
